Organizing Committee
BERC China Focus was founded to create a student community to educate and empower those who want to make a difference in China and beyond. With established relationships with international non-profits and companies, and the experience of organizing a panel for the largest student-run energy conference in the United States, BERC China Focus pushes to become the go-to resource for anyone interested in the environment and China

In the past few years, the United States and China are leading the global energy and environment movement. Recent developments have revealed difficulties that come with collaboration efforts between the two juggernauts, often caused by lack of cultural understanding and channels for open thought and technology exchange.

While difficulties exist, the energy industry will only become more globalized in the future, favoring those who are prepared with collaboration skills, cultural understanding, and developed networks. No doubt, the young generation from US and China will be at the center of globalization.

Student Presentation
Final presentation and Energy and Environment Forum will take place at Anna Head Alumnae Hall, UC Berkeley, at a location that is center to student life. Built in 1927 and covers approximately 6,500 square feet, Anna Head Alumnae Hall was built is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.

At our opening session, leaders in our prestigious partner institutions will share their insights and inspiration for our energy and environment future. The most exciting moment of the whole competition – the final round presentation – will be our key event at the forum. Afterwards, competing teams will receive professional comments from expert judges and awards will be announced!

Workshop
The Energy and Environment Workshop provides customized enrichment sessions to our highly motivated and achieving students. We strive to provide a comprehensive workshop that builds up on the competition topics. Water, energy, air, city and economics are all in our scope.

Learning with such a small group of people (our finalist teams) means all of the participants will have opportunity to talk with our energy and environment experts, getting individual advice and discussing their research interest in the school. The workshop is designed to help participants get a close education opportunity, a combination that will be hard to find elsewhere.
